About sclamp


sclamp is unordinary search engine that drives Aha! moments happen.
Aha! moment is also called as Eureka moment, that is when a great idea comes up.
When you search words, it won't search the way it should,
on the other hands, it searchs the words in the opposite directions or other directions.


for example, when you search the word 'good',
it searchs 'bad' or 'acceptable' or 'great'.
This search engine tries to find the opposite, near, close results from the words(ideally one word) you type.
if it does not get any creative results, it alteratively gives you the usual search engine result.


the moment a great idea comes up in one's brain is often
when getting to know, experience, or learn two or more really different things that he or she never expected.
this search engine gives you chances to make that happen
by meeting many unexpected news and pages.


the way to get the best result from the search is,

-FIRST, sign up your account for 1 minute(with only your name and email address)
-SECOND, search the words that is related to a topic you are trying to come up with an idea
-THIRD, on each searching, save searched items that give you a good knowledge after reading news/page.
-FOURTH, occasionally, visit your account page and read through the saved items and put those summary contents in your brain_map note.

the service is currently under development and DEMO&Trial Phase.
So, except a simple word, the opposite result won't show and the ordinary results are shown.
We will nortify as soon as the innovative AI system is introduced and the real opposite result starts showing up!!
